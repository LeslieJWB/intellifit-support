## Support

### Contact

For help with your account, bugs, subscriptions, or data requests:

**Email:** [support@yourdomain.com](mailto:support@yourdomain.com)

We aim to respond within a few business days.

### What we can help with

- Sign-in issues (email, Sign in with Apple, Google)
- Password reset or email confirmation
- Workout or nutrition logging problems
- AI features (daily limits, errors, or inaccurate estimates)
- Subscriptions and billing (App Store / IntelliFit Pro)
- Privacy requests (access or deletion of your data)

### Sign-in

IntelliFit requires an account. Supported sign-in methods:

- Email and password (via Supabase Auth)
- Sign in with Apple
- Google

### Subscriptions

IntelliFit is free to download. Optional **IntelliFit Pro** subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Manage or cancel subscriptions in **Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions** on your device.

Subscription status may be verified through [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/) to enable subscriber features.

### AI features

Free accounts have a limited number of AI requests per day (food analysis, workout suggestions, summaries, and feedback). Subscribers receive a higher daily limit. AI output is informational only and is not medical or dietary advice.

### Health disclaimer

IntelliFit is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Nutrition estimates from photos or text are approximations. Consult a qualified professional for health decisions.

### 2. Information we collect

#### Account information

When you create an account, we collect:

- Email address (and authentication metadata from your sign-in provider)
- User identifier from our authentication provider
- Sign-in method (email, Apple, or Google)

Authentication is provided by **[Supabase](https://supabase.com/)**.

#### Profile and fitness data you provide

You may provide:

- Display name or username
- Date of birth, gender, height
- Default body weight and body fat percentage
- Daily calorie, protein, and fat targets
- Optional custom text used to personalize AI prompts

#### Workout and activity data

- Workout dates and themes
- Exercises, sets, reps, and weights
- Notes and training history used for statistics and AI coaching features

#### Nutrition data

- Food descriptions you enter manually
- Optional food photos you choose to submit for AI analysis
- Estimated calories, protein, fat, and related AI-generated comments
- Input mode (text only, image only, or both)

**Note:** Food photos are sent to our servers for AI analysis. Our database stores the food description, nutrition estimates, and image MIME type; we do not intend to retain the raw photo file in our database after analysis is complete. Photos are transmitted to third-party AI providers as described below.

#### Body metrics

- Body weight and optional body fat percentage by date

#### Subscription and usage data

- Subscription status and entitlement information (via Apple and [RevenueCat](https://www.revenuecat.com/))
- Daily AI usage counts and quota tier (free, subscriber, or unlimited where applicable)

#### Device permissions

With your permission:

- **Camera** — to photograph food for optional nutrition analysis
- **Photo library** — to select existing food photos for optional analysis

Workout logging does not require camera access.

#### Data we do not collect

We do not knowingly collect:

- Precise location data
- Contacts from your address book
- Health data from Apple Health / HealthKit (unless you add such integration in a future version)
